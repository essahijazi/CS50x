Nissan - Innovation That Excites

Nissan Motor Co., Ltd., founded in 1933, is renowned for its innovation in electric vehicles and performance-oriented cars.

History

Nissan began as Datsun in the early 20th century and evolved into one of Japan's leading automakers, producing iconic models such as the Nissan GT-R and Nissan Fairlady Z.

Popular Models

Nissan GT-R

The Nissan GT-R, also known as Godzilla, is a high-performance sports car celebrated for its speed and handling.

Nissan 350Z

The Nissan 350Z (Fairlady Z in Japan) is a legendary sports car known for its sleek design and exhilarating performance.

Global Influence

Nissan's commitment to electric vehicles and innovative technologies has positioned it as a leader in the global automotive market, shaping future mobility solutions.
